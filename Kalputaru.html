Kalpataru Institute of Technology - KIT, Tiptur

Established Year: 1986

Affiliation: VTU, Belgaum

Approved: AICTE, NBA

Address: NH-206, Vidya Nagar, Tiptur, Karnataka 572201

Highest Package: 15.0 LPA

Average Package: 4.0 LPA


BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration):


Admission Eligibility: H.S.C. examination passed from any Stream with English as a passing Subject.

Course Highlights: BBA, a three-year undergraduate course, is a gateway to numerous job opportunities in a multitude of sectors like Marketing, Finance, Sales, HR, Education, Government Etc. This course provide education & training in management to enhance the leadership quality to pursue a career in the corporate management sector.


List of Courses Available:

  • B.Tech - Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.Tech - Information Science and Engineering
  • B.Tech - Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech - Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech - Civil Engineering
  • MBA - Master of Business Administration

History and achievements: Kalpataru Institute of Technology was established in 1986 with a view to provide quality technical education. The college is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum, approved by AICTE and accredited by NBA, New Delhi. The college has well-qualified and experienced staff, spacious classrooms, well-equipped labs, and dedicated faculty.
