History and Achievement:

Kalpataru Institute of Technology was established in 1986 with a view to provide quality technical education. The college is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum, approved by AICTE and accredited by NBA, New Delhi. The college has well-qualified and experienced staff, spacious classrooms, well-equipped labs, and dedicated faculty.
