About Tagore Education Consultants

Welcome to Tagore Education Consultants, a premier student consultancy firm located in the heart of Dhaka, Bangladesh. Our mission is to address the educational challenges of today's globalized world by bridging the gap between the education systems of Bangladesh and those of developed countries. We foster cross-cultural understanding and collaboration, positioning ourselves as the top education consultancy firm in Bangladesh.

At Tagore Student Consultants, we provide comprehensive and efficient guidance for studying abroad, empowering students to build active and successful professional careers. Our commitment to personalized attention and a solid foundation ensures that each student receives the support they need to thrive academically and professionally.

As one of the leading study abroad agencies in Bangladesh, we facilitate placements in universities and colleges worldwide. Our close partnerships with students and institutions guarantee that every student receives accurate and tailored advice. Our extensive knowledge of global educational institutions, coupled with our strong relationships with them, ensures optimal placement opportunities for our students.

In addition to our educational services, we excel in immigration consultancy. We assist skilled individuals, families, and business professionals in emigrating from Bangladesh to countries such as the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Italy, Norway, Hungary, Romania, and Dubai. For your peace of mind, all immigration advice is provided in writing. We manage the entire application process, including residence visa processing, business migration, job search, qualification and trades recognition, and resettlement services. We also handle visitor visas, making us the best immigration consultancy firm in Dhaka.

Our reputation is built on the satisfaction of our clients, many of whom return for additional services and recommend us to others. We take pride in the testimonials we receive and strive to achieve even greater success in the years to come.

Join us at Tagore Education Consultants and experience our exceptional services firsthand. If you have any questions or need further information, please do not hesitate to contact our friendly staff by phone or email. We look forward to helping you achieve your educational and immigration goals.
